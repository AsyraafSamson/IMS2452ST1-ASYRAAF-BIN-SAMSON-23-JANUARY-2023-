Source of income: The Organization's financial resources are undetermined because it is a volunteer organization. The majority of RBDH's income comes from:

Receiving from generous people and corporate bodies.

Approximately 5% (per year) of the total amount is allocated to orphans by the central government. The Department of Social Welfare is the conduit for this donation (JKM). (The donation amount in 2020 is RM 57,000.00).

Savings or surpluses acquired from public donations made throughout the month of Ramadhan are used to replace the shortfall in revenue from costs.

Aspects of RBDH Expenses:

Electricity, water and gas supply (4.4%).

Food and beverage supplies (9.7%).

Oil and vehicle maintenance (1 bus and 2 vans) – (3.0%).

Teaching allowance for religious teachers & tuition fees (6.4%).

Building repair and maintenance (5.0%).

Purchase of books and stationery (1.5%).

School allowance for children in day school (15%).

Employee wages (29%).

Telephone expenses (0.5%).

Various (Astro, Sokeso, Medicine, Clothing) – (25.5%).
